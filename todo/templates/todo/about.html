About

Welcome to our to-do list application! With our app, you can manage your tasks and stay organized.

Features

  • Create and manage multiple to-do lists
  • Add delete tasks
  • Access your to-do lists from anywhere
  • View and update account information

Admin Features

  • Authentication and authorization
  • CRUD operations
  • Automatic form generation
  • List and detail views
  • Search and filtering functionality

User Authentication

Our application provides user authentication to ensure the privacy and security of your to-do lists. By registering an account and logging in, you can access your personalized to-do lists and keep your tasks private.

User authentication allows you to:

  • Create a unique account
  • Login securely with your credentials
  • Reset your password if needed
  • Protect your tasks and data from unauthorized access
  • Review your profile and change

Objective

Our team is dedicated to providing a user-friendly and efficient to-do list application. We strive to help individuals and teams stay organized, manage their tasks, and increase productivity.
